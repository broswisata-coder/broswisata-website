# Instagram Profile Audit - @broswisata

Audit date: 2026-07-02

Screenshot context:
- Handle: @broswisata
- Profile name: BROS WISATA | Hub Of Sumatra
- Category: Travel Company
- Current public numbers visible in screenshot: 126 posts, 35 followers, 29 following
- Bio focus: Explore North Sumatra, Lake Toba, Bukit Lawang, Berastagi, WhatsApp

## CTO Recommendation

The account is valid as the official BROS Wisata channel. The foundation is already there: brand name, logo, destination keywords, WhatsApp, and existing post volume.

Main issue: profile currently feels like a general travel page. It should feel like a trusted private North Sumatra tour specialist led by local field knowledge.

## Priority 1 - Bio

Recommended bio:

```text
BROS WISATA | Hub of Sumatra
Private North Sumatra tours
Lake Toba | Bukit Lawang | Berastagi
Plan your trip with Ahmad
```

If there is enough space, use:

```text
BROS WISATA | Hub of Sumatra
Private North Sumatra tours from Medan
Lake Toba | Bukit Lawang | Berastagi
Custom quote by WhatsApp
```

Why:
- "Private" attracts Europe/Singapore custom-trip travelers.
- "from Medan" helps search intent.
- "Ahmad" adds human trust.
- "Custom quote" avoids cheap-package positioning.
