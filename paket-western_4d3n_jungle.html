Welcome to North Sumatra! Upon arrival at Kualanamu International Airport (KNO), your English-speaking guide will greet you. Scenic drive passing rubber and palm oil plantations to Bukit Lawang, a village at the edge of Gunung Leuser National Park. Check into your eco-lodge beside the Bohorok River and relax.

Trekking Orangutan & Tube Rafting Trekking Orangutan & Tube Rafting Orangutan Jungle Trek & Tube Rafting

Setelah sarapan, trekking setengah hari ke hutan hujan. Dengarkan suara alam dan cari monyet daun Thomas, owa, dan orangutan Sumatra semi-liar yang megah. Setelah trekking, nikmati tube rafting tradisional kembali ke lodge. Sore bebas untuk berenang atau bersantai. Selepas sarapan, trekking separuh hari ke hutan hujan. Dengar bunyi alam dan cari monyet daun Thomas, ungka, dan orangutan Sumatera separa-liar. Selepas trekking, nikmati tube rafting tradisional kembali ke lodge. Petang bebas untuk berenang atau santai. After breakfast, begin a half-day jungle trek into the rainforest. Listen to the sounds of nature and look out for Thomas Leaf monkeys, gibbons, and the majestic semi-wild Sumatran Orangutan. After the trek, experience a fun traditional tube rafting ride down the river back to your lodge. The rest of the afternoon is free at leisure.

Tangkahan Elephant Sanctuary – Medan Tangkahan Elephant Sanctuary – Medan Tangkahan Elephant Sanctuary – Medan

Hari ini kita off-road ke Tangkahan, dikenal sebagai "Hidden Paradise." Nikmati pengalaman luar biasa membantu mahout memandikan gajah Sumatra yang diselamatkan di sungai. Setelahnya, kembali ke Medan City. Check-in hotel dan nikmati kuliner Medan di malam hari. Hari ini off-road ke Tangkahan, dikenali sebagai "Hidden Paradise." Pengalaman luar biasa membantu mahout memandikan gajah Sumatera yang diselamatkan di sungai. Selepas itu, kembali ke Medan. Daftar masuk hotel dan nikmati kuliner Medan di malam hari. Today we drive off-road to Tangkahan, famously known as the "Hidden Paradise." Enjoy an incredible experience watching and helping mahouts wash the rescued Sumatran elephants in the river. Afterwards, drive back to Medan City. Check into your hotel and enjoy the city's vibrant culinary scene at night.

Depending on your flight schedule, our driver will transfer you to Kualanamu Airport for your onward journey.
