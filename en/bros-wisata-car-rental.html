✦ Before booking

Important details to know.

Do you offer self-drive rental?

This page is for chauffeured vehicle service. If your requirement is different, message the team so they can check the correct option without promising a unit in advance.

Are fuel, tolls, parking, and overtime included?

Do not assume they are automatically included. The written quote will state the vehicle, driver, fuel, tolls or parking, operating-hour limit, overtime, and driver accommodation where relevant.

How do I choose the right vehicle?

Send the number of guests, luggage quantity and size, route, and comfort preference. We recommend for comfortable capacity, not simply the maximum number of seats.

Will the exact vehicle match the image?

The images are official model illustrations. The actual model, variant, colour, year, and seating configuration depend on availability and are confirmed in writing before payment.

Can you arrange a Kualanamu airport pick-up?

It can be arranged with advance booking. Send the flight number, arrival time, lead passenger name, destination, guest count, and luggage.

What is the change or cancellation policy?

The applicable terms follow the written booking confirmation and current BROS Terms & Conditions. Please review them before paying a deposit.
