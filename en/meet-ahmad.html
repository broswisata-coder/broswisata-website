Places Ahmad knows through real field work.

The strongest routes are not just beautiful on paper. They have known road time, reliable local contacts, and sensible rest stops.

Bukit Lawang

Orangutan trekking routes with local jungle guide partners and slower pacing for responsible wildlife viewing.

Tangkahan

River, rainforest, and village routes arranged with local partners and realistic travel time from Medan.

Berastagi

Highland weather, fruit market stops, volcano views, and Sipiso-Piso timing are planned around the group.

Lake Toba

Samosir ferry timing, hotel category, view stops, and long-road breaks are checked before quotation.

A good Sumatra tour is not about rushing to every place. It is about knowing which roads, stops, and people make the trip feel safe and alive.

Tell Ahmad your travel date, number of guests, hotel style, and places you want to see. His team will shape a private route with a fair quotation.
