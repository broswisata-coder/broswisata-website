📅 FULL ITINERARY

1
DAY 1
ARRIVAL MEDAN → BUKIT LAWANG
  • →
    Arrival at Kualanamu International Airport.
  • →
    Drive to Bukit Lawang (approx. 4 hours) through palm oil plantations.
  • →
    Check-in at riverside eco-lodge by the Bahorok River.
  • →
    Briefing session with licensed jungle guide.
  • →
    Overnight in Bukit Lawang.
2
DAY 2
RAINFOREST TREKKING
  • →
    Breakfast at lodge.
  • →
    Half-day jungle trek (3–4 hours) into Gunung Leuser National Park.
  • →
    Chance to encounter orangutans, siamangs, langurs, and Thomas leaf monkeys.
  • →
    Jungle picnic lunch in the forest.
  • →
    Return to lodge. Afternoon swim in the river.
  • →
    Overnight in Bukit Lawang.
3
DAY 3
TUBING & BAT CAVE
  • →
    Breakfast at lodge.
  • →
    River tubing on the Bahorok River — safe, fun adrenaline activity.
  • →
    Visit Bat Cave (Gua Kelawar).
  • →
    Afternoon at leisure in Bukit Lawang village. Optional BBQ fish dinner.
  • →
    Overnight in Bukit Lawang.
4
DAY 4
BUKIT LAWANG → DEPARTURE
  • →
    Breakfast at lodge.
  • →
    Drive back to Medan.
  • →
    Souvenir stop before transfer to airport.
  • →
    End of services.
