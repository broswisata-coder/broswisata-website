The Ultimate North Sumatra Eco-Tour

The signature seven-day eco-tour combines a Tangkahan rainforest route with distance-based elephant viewing when available, orangutan trekking, a volcano hike, and Lake Toba's Samosir Island.

"The complete experience: Tangkahan, Bukit Lawang, Berastagi, and Lake Toba"

📅 FULL ITINERARY

1
DAY 1
Arrival – Tangkahan Eco-Tourism

Arrive at Kualanamu Airport. Transfer directly to Tangkahan, a remote village at the rainforest edge. Check into your jungle lodge and rest.

2
DAY 2
Tangkahan Conservation Route – Off-road to Bukit Lawang

If elephant conservation activity is underway, observe from a safe distance with no guest participation. Guests do not feed, bathe, ride, touch, or make direct contact with elephants. Afterwards, take a 4WD jeep ride through plantation tracks to Bukit Lawang. Check into your riverside accommodation.

3
DAY 3
Bukit Lawang Orangutan Trek

Enter Gunung Leuser National Park for a half-day trek with a chance to observe Sumatran orangutans in natural habitat. Wildlife sightings are never guaranteed; the guide adjusts the route and respectful viewing distance to conditions on the day. Conclude the trek with a river tube ride back to your lodge.

4
DAY 4
Bukit Lawang – Berastagi

Leave the rainforest behind and travel to the Karo Highlands (Berastagi). Visit the traditional market and enjoy the cool mountain breeze.

5
DAY 5
Volcano Trek – Lake Toba (Samosir Island)

Early morning hike up Mt. Sibayak volcano. After descending, drive towards Lake Toba, stopping at the spectacular 120m Sipiso-piso Waterfall. Take a ferry across the crater lake to Samosir Island and check into your lakeside resort.

6
DAY 6
Samosir Island Exploration

A full day to explore the island. Visit the ancient Batak stone chairs at Ambarita and traditional villages. You can also rent a bicycle or simply swim in the refreshing waters of Lake Toba.

7
DAY 7
Lake Toba – Medan Departure

Take the ferry back to the mainland (Parapat) and drive to Kualanamu Airport for your flight out, bringing home unforgettable memories of Sumatra.
