Planning Answers

Before you ask for a quote,
these points matter.

Private North Sumatra trips change with date, comfort level, route, and group size. We keep the first conversation focused on fit, safety, and route quality before sending a fair quotation.

Why is the tour price by request?

Because the final cost depends on travel date, hotel category, number of guests, route distance, guide needs, and transport choice. Ahmad's team checks the plan first so the quote is realistic.

Can you arrange eco tours for European guests?

Yes. Bukit Lawang, Tangkahan, Berastagi, and Lake Toba can be shaped as private eco routes with local guide partners, responsible wildlife distance, and flexible pacing.

Do you serve Malaysia and Singapore travelers?

Yes. We arrange halal-friendly stops, tour transport planning, Kualanamu arrival planning, family routes, and quotation support for guests from Malaysia and Singapore.
