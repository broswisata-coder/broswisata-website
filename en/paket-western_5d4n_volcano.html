📅 FULL ITINERARY

1
DAY 1
Kualanamu Airport – Bukit Lawang

Arrival at Kualanamu Airport. Meet your guide and drive to the jungle village of Bukit Lawang. Settle into your riverside eco-lodge and enjoy the serene rainforest atmosphere.

2
DAY 2
Bukit Lawang Jungle Trekking

Embark on a guided half-day trek into the Gunung Leuser National Park. Your local ranger will help you spot Orangutans, macaques, and exotic birds. Return to the lodge via an exciting river tube ride. Free afternoon to swim or relax.

3
DAY 3
Bukit Lawang – Berastagi Highlands

Drive up the winding roads to the cool climate of Berastagi in the Karo Highlands. Visit the vibrant traditional fruit and flower market, and marvel at the golden replica of the Shwedagon Pagoda (Lumbini Park). Check into your guesthouse.

4
DAY 4
Mt. Sibayak Volcano Trek – Medan

Wake up early for a sunrise trek up Mount Sibayak, an active volcano. Enjoy the breathtaking views of the crater and surrounding valleys. After descending, relax your muscles in a local natural hot spring before driving back to Medan City for your final night.

5
DAY 5
Departure Medan

Breakfast at the hotel. Free at leisure until your transfer to Kualanamu International Airport for your departure flight.
